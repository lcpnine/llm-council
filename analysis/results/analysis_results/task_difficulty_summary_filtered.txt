REVISED CONCLUSIONS:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Multi-agent debate shows DATASET-SPECIFIC effects:

✓ MCQ Datasets (MedQA, MMLU):
  - Debate IMPROVES accuracy by 7-8%
  - Beneficial for well-defined multiple-choice questions

✗ PubMedQA (yes/no/maybe):
  - Debate REDUCES decisive accuracy by ~35%
  - BUT improves uncertainty detection (maybe recall +323%)
  - Trade-off: decisive answers vs. uncertainty recognition

★ Difficulty-Dependent:
  - Most effective on genuinely hard questions
  - Can introduce unnecessary doubt on easy questions
